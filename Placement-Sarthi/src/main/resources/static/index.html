About Graphic Era Hill University

Premier institution committed to academic excellence and holistic student development

Graphic Era Hill University (GEHU) is a premier institution committed to academic excellence and holistic student development. With a strong focus on innovation, skill-building, and industry collaboration, GEHU provides its students with ample opportunities to excel in their careers.

The Placement Portal is an extension of this vision, connecting students with top companies and fostering career growth through a streamlined process that benefits both students and recruiters.

Our Placement Process

Simple and efficient steps to connect students with their dream careers

1

Registration

Students register on the portal and create their profiles with academic and skills information.

2

Profile Screening

Our team screens student profiles to ensure they meet company requirements.

3

Company Shortlisting

Companies review student profiles and shortlist candidates for further rounds.

4

Interviews & Selection

Shortlisted students undergo interviews and selection processes with companies.

5

Offer & Onboarding

Selected students receive offers and begin their professional journey.
